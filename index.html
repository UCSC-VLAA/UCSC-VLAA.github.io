Our projects

Our lab is in the Computer Science and Engineering Department, UC Santa Cruz.
With the goal of building human-level computer vision systems,
our lab has a broad interest in computer vision applications with real-world challenges,
including but not limited to adversirial training, reinforcement learning, and medical image computing.
