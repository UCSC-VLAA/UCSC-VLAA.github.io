Welcome to VLAA lab


Vision · Learning · Assured Autonomy

Our projects

Our lab has a broad interest in computer vision and machine learning, including developing efficient deep representation learning with minimal supervision and securing model performance under (adversarial) distribution shifts. In addition, we are interested in building transparent and trustworthy medical AI systems that can function effectively in complex clinical environments.
